Dancing has so many health benefits. It's a great outlet for stress, builds strength and benefits the brain in improving memory and balance. Laughter and social connection in bringing a group of people together to dance are added perks! Belly dancing and Bollywood have expanded my appreciation for uplifting world music!

Bring Women Together
Community Group (BWT)

Bring Women Together Community Group (BWT) was founded in 2007 by a community-minded group of women who wanted to create a local venue to dance, develop new skills and get some exercise in a supportive atmosphere.

Over the years, the group has seen women gain confidence, make new friends, build relationships, enhance existing relationships with friends and co-workers, get fit and flexible, share cultures and above all do something fun for themselves!

This dance class was originally designed as a school council fundraiser for Timberbank Junior Public School. The group is now a non-profit organization providing dance instruction in both belly dance and popular Bollywood styles to adults of all ages and backgrounds. Instruction includes a warm up and warm down, skill development and choreography. No previous experience is necessary.
